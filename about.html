IA Computer Lab

My name is Tyler Maxwell and I will be your new computer lab assistant. I taught Secondary Social Science for about seven years. However, I have never worked with students below the 6th grade. I would love your feedback and suggestions about how to incorporate computer programming instruction into your curriculum. The last thing I want to be is a distraction.

My vision is to help students incorporate technology into what they are currently learning. Some of the ideas I have are:

  • websites for history or ela projects
  • Python scripts to visualize mathematical concepts
  • Using Scratch for just about anything!

Again, I have never worked with this age group, so feel free to add suggestions or reign me in if I am getting too far from reality.
